Presentation by: Patrick Little

Zoomy.js
http://www.jameslouiz.com/experiment/zoomy/

Zoomy.js is a plug-in that allows you to zoom into a picture to see a close up portion of it. There are many different ways to zoom in. There is a default zoom which I will be showing you. There is also an innerzoom. There are plenty of options to tinker with. When you hover your mouse over the picture a display box will appear of the zoomed image. You can resize the display box if you don't like the default size. You can use the magnify property to zoom in even more. You also can use fade durations and trigger events.
